EASY BREEZY BANANA SMOOTHIE!!!

Description

Nutritious and delicious smoothie that is filling and perfect for people on the go.

Ingredients

  1. 1 1/2 cups of almond milk
  2. 1 large or 2 small bananas
  3. 1/4 cup of peanut butter or almond butter
  4. 1-2 scoops of protein powder
  5. 1/4 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  6. 1 cup of crushed ice
  7. 1-2 handfuls of fresh baby spinach

    8. Great Add-In Ingredients!

    • Agave
    • Chia Seeds
    • Hemp Seeds
    • Cocoa Powder
    • avocado

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients into a blender
  2. Blend until all elements are in a smooth texture
  3. Serve Immediately
